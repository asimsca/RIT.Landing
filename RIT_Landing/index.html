About Us

Established in 2020, Raj Integrated Technologies is a trusted name in the healthcare and technology sector. Based in Peshawar, we provide advanced medical, laboratory, surgical, IT, and industrial solutions across Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and beyond. With a dedicated team of skilled professionals and strong partnerships with leading international manufacturers, we deliver reliable products and tailored services that meet the evolving needs of hospitals, laboratories, and institutions. Our commitment to innovation, integrity, and excellence drives us to enhance patient care, support research advancements, and strengthen healthcare infrastructure across Pakistan.

  • Hospitals & Medical Institutions

    Serving government, semi-government, and private hospitals with complete solutions for operating theatres, ICUs, wards, and diagnostic departments.

  • Laboratories & Research Centers

    Supplying reliable laboratory instruments, pathology items, and scientific equipment that support medical research and clinical testing.

  • Corporate & NGO Sector

    Providing industrial supplies, IT infrastructure, and general order solutions to private organizations, NGOs, and research institutions nationwide.

  • Educational & Training Institutions

    Partnering with universities and medical colleges to equip laboratories and training centers with modern teaching and diagnostic technologies.

  • Industrial & IT Solutions

    Delivering comprehensive IT systems, network infrastructure, and industrial tools to enhance operational performance and digital transformation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and support process.

What types of medical and laboratory equipment do you provide?

We offer a wide range of equipment including surgical instruments, diagnostic devices, laboratory analyzers, hospital furniture, and IT infrastructure solutions tailored for healthcare institutions.

Do you supply to both government and private hospitals?

Yes. We work with government, semi-government, and private hospitals across Pakistan, delivering complete turnkey solutions for operating theatres, ICUs, and diagnostic departments.

Do you provide installation and maintenance services?

Absolutely. Our technical team ensures proper installation, calibration, and after-sales maintenance to keep your equipment performing efficiently.

Are your products internationally certified?

Yes, we collaborate with globally recognized manufacturers and ensure all products meet international standards such as ISO, CE, and FDA certifications.

Do you offer customized solutions for specific institutions?

Yes, we tailor our product and service offerings to match the specific needs of hospitals, laboratories, and industrial clients — ensuring complete satisfaction and efficiency.

How can I request a quotation or product catalog?

You can reach us directly via our contact form or email. Our sales team will provide a detailed quotation and product catalog based on your requirements within 24–48 hours.
